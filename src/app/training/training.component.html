Training and development

Superior HealthCare strives to create a working environment supported by a highly trained agency staff. We ensure that agency staff working for Superior Healthcare have the skills and training required to provide high standard of care to our clients.

We ensure that staff employed by Superior Health care have completed the statutory and mandatory training such as:

  • Lone working
  • RIDDOR
  • COSHH
  • Food Safety & Hygiene level 2
  • Mental Capacity Act 2005 and Deprivation of Liberty Safeguard (Dols)
  • Basic Life Support
  • Moving and handling
  • Infection Control
  • Information Governance
